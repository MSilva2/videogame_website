Video Game Design

When designing video games, just like every other type of design, the necessary steps to follow often include:
  • Brainstorming,
  • Research,
  • Sketching,
  • Backgrounds, Music, Sound and Character Design,
  • Programming.

Ways to Start

There are two common ways to start in Video Game Design:
  1. Going through the process steps, one by one
  2. Perfecting one step at a time
